Professor

SERGIO MARQUINA better known as THE PROFESSOR, is one of the main protagonists, as well as the brain behind the heist at the Royal Mint of Spain and the Bank of Spain

Tokyo

TOKYO, is one of the main protagonists, as well as narrator of the story. She is a member of the heist crew set to rob the Royal Mint of Spain.

Berlin

BERLIN, he is a main character, he is a terminally ill jewel thief and the Professor's second-in-command and brother.

Nairobi

NAIROBI, is one of the main protagonists, as well as one of the robbers that participated in heist of the Royal Mint of Spain.

Denver

DENVER, is one of the main protagonists, as well as a robber that participated in the Royal Mint of Spain.

Lisbon

LISBON, is one of the main protagonists, as well as a Spanish police inspector, who was tasked with leading the police team at the Heist at the Royal Mint of Spain.

Helsinki

HELSINKI, is one of the main protagonists, as well as a brute that doesn't say much but lets his actions do the talking.

Rio

RIO, is one of the main protagonists, as well as a hacker and a member of a group who robbed the Royal Mint of Spain.
